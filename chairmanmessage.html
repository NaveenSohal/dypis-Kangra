Chairman's message

  • Chairman's message

Dear Parents, It is with great pleasure that we welcome you and your child to the DY Patil International School. We will do our best to assist you and your child to settle quickly into our community, and to help him/ her make the most of this educational opportunity.

Your children are the most precious resource within our community and we need to nurture them by providing the very best education possible. We believe that this means offering a vibrant, innovative and rigorous education, aligned with the International Baccalaureate and University of Cambridge Programmes. The curriculum inspires and challenges our students to develop a love for learning and ensures that they have the knowledge, skills and values to cope with a rapidly changing world.

Our mission statement clearly identifies the importance we place on inspiring young minds and challenging young individuals within a caring international environment. As part of our community of learners, your children will experience a warm and welcoming environment and gain a sense of confidence from the highly skilled and professional staff working here at the school.

DYPIS is a happy, exciting place filled with children learning the academic, personal and social skills necessary for life-long learning, success and happiness.

At DYPIS, education is viewed as a partnership in which Parents and the School work closely together to ensure that our young people are able to be confident. knowledgeable, responsible, caring and capable citizens. Throughout the year, we look forward to meeting with parents, both formally and informally, as we share in the holistic development of our students.

We welcome you to actively participate in making our mission statement a reality!


With Warm Regards,
Dr Ajeenkya DY Patil Chairman
